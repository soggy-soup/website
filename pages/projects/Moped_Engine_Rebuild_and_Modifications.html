Moped Engine Rebuild and Modifications

10.12.2021 - Current

I bought a 1999 Tomos Targa LX moped which I use for commuting. I rebuilt the engine 2 years ago and have been riding it since. Besides general maintenance, I fabricated a custom intake to mate with an aftermarket carb, made a custom kickstand system, and rebuilt the 2-speed centrifugal clutch.
